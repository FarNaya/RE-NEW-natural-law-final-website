WHO WE ARE

Natural Law Energy has been created as a covalition of First Nations in Saskatchewan and Alberta to pursue economic opportunities for the wealth and benefit of Indigenous Peoples. We are an economic alliance of the original Nations of Treaty No. 4, Treaty No. 6 and Treaty No. 7 territories. NLE is pursuing intergenerational wealth and benefit for our Peoples and Nations. We follow our traditional governance to create prosperity, health and success for the next seven generations. We believe in our natural laws as Indigenous People.
